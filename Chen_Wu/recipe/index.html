Chocolate Peanut Butter Cup Cookies Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter softened
  • 3/4 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 1/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup peanut butter chips
  • 10 chocolate covered peanut butter cups, cut into eighths

Nutrition

  • Calories: 230
  • Total Fat: 13g
  • Cholesterol: 26mg

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, peanut butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. Combine the flour, cocoa, and baking soda; stir into the peanut butter mixture.
  5. Mix in the chocolate chips, peanut butter chips, and peanut butter cups
  6. Drop by tablespoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  7. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven.
  8. Let cool for 1 or 2 minutes on sheet before removing, or they will fall apart.

Green Tea (Matcha) Ice Cream Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk (480ml)
  • 2 Tbsp green tea powder (Matcha)
  • 1 cup heavy cream (240ml)
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup and 2 Tbsp sugar (120g)

Instructions

  1. Place ice water in a large bowl that can hold a pot. Set aside.
  2. In a small bowl, add 1/4 cup of milk and green tea powder, and whisk well (the powder may not dissolve completely). T
  3. Add them back to the rest of milk (1 3/4 cups) and mix.
  4. Heat the milk mixture and 1/2 cup of heavy cream at medium heat, and cook until just before boiling.
  5. In a medium bowl, whisk the rest of the heavy cream (1/2 cup), yolks, and sugar.
  6. Add hot milk very slowly to the egg mixture.
  7. Put egg/milk mixture back to the pot and cook at medium heat stirring constantly until thick (to check the thickness, dip a wood spoon in the egg/milk mixture, and check and see if you can scrape a clear line on the back of the spoon with a finger).
  8. Place the pot in the ice water and cool.
  9. Transfer to a container and chill at least 3 hours in the refrigerator.
  10. Follow the direction of your ice cream machine and mix. Very soft ice cream is done after about 15-20 minutes.
  11. Freeze 3 hours before serving.
