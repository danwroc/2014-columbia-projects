Miley Cyrus

Miley Cyrus isn't the first celebrity to find navigating the road from squeaky clean child star to poised adult actor difficult. But she is the first to do it with a posse of dancers in bear costumes behind her.

The path from Disney tween princess to MTV infamous

  • 1992: Miley is born Destiny Hope Cyrus. It's unclear then that her destiny will involve twerking on television.
  • 2006: Miley gets her big break on Disney Channel's Hannah Montana
  • 2007: Releases her first album Meet Miley Cyrus
  • 2008: Poses with a bedsheet for a Vanity Fair photoshoot with Annie Leibowitz; causes an uproar
  • 2008: Releases her second album Breakout
  • 2009: Stars in a movie, launches a clothing line, and writes a memoir. Surprisingly, the book has multiple chapters.
  • 2011: A video of Miley smoking a bong surfaces online. Dad Billy Ray Cyrus tells GQ that the Disney channel is the reason his family is under attack from Satan and compares Miley to Anna Nicole Smith
  • 2011: Hosts SNL; Critics call her performance pretty funny
  • 2012: Shaves her head; comparisons to Britney Spears follow
  • 2013: Releases the album Bangerz and the single Wrecking Ball becomes a huge hit. The best thing to come from it is this.
  • 2013: Miley dances with, among other things: fake bears, a giant foam finger, Robin Thicke, a cat crying diamonds; sets off media frenzy, widespread headshaking
