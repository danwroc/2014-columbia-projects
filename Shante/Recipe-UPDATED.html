Chocolate Peanut Butter Cup Cookies


Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • softened 3/4 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 1/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup peanut butter chips
  • 10 chocolate covered peanut butter cups

Directions

Step 1: In a large bowl, cream together the butter, peanut butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
Step 2: Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
Step 3: Combine the flour, cocoa, and baking soda; stir into the peanut butter mixture.
Step 4: Mix in the chocolate chips, peanut butter chips, and peanut butter cups.
Step 5: Drop by tablespoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.
Step 6: Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven.
Final Step: Let cool for 1 or 2 minutes on sheet before removing, or they will fall apart.

Calories: 230 | Total Fat: 13g | Cholesterol: 26mg Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

Chocolate Chip Oatmeal Cookie


Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup(s) (packed) brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup(s) granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup(s) trans fat-free vegetable oil spread (60% to 70% oil)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 large egg white
  • 2 teaspoon(s) vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cup(s) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon(s) baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 2 1/2 cup(s) quick-cooking or old-fashioned oats, uncooked
  • 1 cup(s) bittersweet (62% cacao) or semisweet chocolate chips

Directions

Step 1: In large bowl, with mixer on medium-low speed, beat sugars and vegetable spread until well blended, occasionally scraping bowl with rubber spatula.
Step 2: Add egg, egg white, and vanilla; beat until smooth. Beat in flour, baking soda, and salt until mixed.
Step 3: With wooden spoon, stir in oats and chocolate chips until well combined.
Step 4: Drop dough by rounded measuring tablespoons, 2 inches apart, on ungreased large cookie sheet.
Step 5: Bake cookies 12 to 13 minutes or until golden.
Final Step: With wide metal spatula, transfer cookies to wire rack to cool.

Calories: 80 | Total Fat: 4g | Cholesterol: 4mg Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
