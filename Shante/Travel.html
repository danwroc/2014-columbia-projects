Feeling Artsy? Explore New York City's Street Art... For Free!

DUMBO Walls

DUMBO walls, located on York and Pearl Streets, near the Brooklyn-Queens Expressway, stretches along 8 walls. Unlike most graffitti art, DUMBO walls is a sponsered project.


Os Gemeos Mural In Coney Island

Alex Webb, born May 5, 1952, is a street photographer and photojournalist. His master understanding of light has allowed him to consistently create photos in intense color. Though he is recognized as a pioneer of American color photography, Webb didn't always shoot in color. He actually began shooting in color in 1978, after working in the Caribbean. The island was so vivacious and full of color that he found it difficult to capture its beauty in black and white.
