Nice and Naughty: Tuesday Night Dinner

Kale and Almond Salad
Ingredients
  • 4-6 cups kale, loosely packed sliced leaves
  • juice of 1 lemon
  • juice of 1 orange
  • zest of 1 orange
  • 3-4 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • cloves garlic, mashed
  • salt & pepper, to taste
  • hot red pepper flakes, to taste
  • 2/3 cup grated Pecorino Toscano cheese or other flavorful grating cheese such as Asiago or Parmesan
  • 1/2 cup of toasted almonds
  • 1/2 cup freshly made bread crumbs from lightly toasted bread
Calories: 202 | Total Fat: 12.17g | Cholesterol: 8.83mg
Method
  1. Whisk together lemon juice, olive oil and orange zest
  2. Add garlic, salt, pepper, and a generous pinch (or more to taste) of hot red pepper flakes to lemon juice and olive oil mixture. Whisk for 30 seconds.
  3. Pour over kale in serving bowl and toss well.
  4. Add 2/3 of the cheese and toss again.
  5. Toss in orange juice
  6. Let kale sit for at least 5 minute.
  7. Add bread crumbs and toasted almonds, toss again.
  8. Top with remaining cheese.
Chocolate Peanut Butter Cup Cookies
Ingredients
  • 1 cup butter softened
  • 3/4 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 1/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup peanut butter chips
  • 10 chocolate covered peanut butter cups, cut into eighths
Calories: 230 | Total Fat: 13g | Cholesterol: 26mg
Method
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, peanut butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. Combine the flour, cocoa, and baking soda; stir into the peanut butter mixture.
  5. Mix in the chocolate chips, peanut butter chips, and peanut butter cups.
  6. Drop by tablespoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  7. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven.
  8. Let cool for 1 or 2 minutes on sheet before removing.
