Chocolate Chip Cookies

4-STEP CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES

What you'll need...

1 cup butter, softened 3/4 cup creamy peanut butter 3/4 cup white sugar
3/4 cup packed brown sugar 2 eggs 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 1/3 cups all-purpose flour 1/3 cup cocoa powder 1 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips 1 cup peanut butter chips 10 chocolate covered peanut butter cups, cut into eighths

**Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).**
Regular

1. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, peanut butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.

2. Combine the flour, cocoa, and baking soda; stir into the peanut butter mixture. Mix in the chocolate chips, peanut butter chips, and peanut butter cups.

3. Drop by tablespoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.

4. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven. Let cool for 1 or 2 minutes on sheet before removing, or they will fall apart.

Gluten-Free

1. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, peanut butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.

2. Combine the blanched almond flour, cocoa, and baking soda; stir into the peanut butter mixture. Mix in the chocolate chips, peanut butter chips, and peanut butter cups.

3. Drop by tablespoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.

4. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven. Let cool for 1 or 2 minutes on sheet before removing, or they will fall apart.
